## Error 1: Incorrect SEBI Regulation Reference

**Location:** Section A5.1 – SEBI IA Regulations 2013

**Error Found:**
> "IA Regulations 2013 (Reg. 18) – Full disclosure of conflicts, fees, risks"

**Correction:**
Section 18 of the SEBI Investment Advisers Regulations 2013 actually deals with **"Maintenance of records"** and **"Periodical submission of information to the Board"**, NOT disclosure of conflicts.

The correct regulation for conflict disclosure is:
- **IA Regulations 2013, Third Schedule (Code of Conduct) – Clause 7:** "An investment adviser shall maintain confidentiality of information and shall not disclose the same to any person unless required to do so by law."

**Impact:** Annotators relying on this incorrect reference would have miscoded compliance checks.

## Error 2: Incorrect RBI Regulation Number

**Location:** Section A5.2 – RBI Digital Lending Guidelines

**Error Found:**
> "Digital Lending Guidelines 2022"

**Correction:**
The correct name is **"RBI Guidelines on Digital Lending"** issued on **September 2, 2022** under the authority of Sections 10(2), 10(4) and 10(5) of the Banking Regulation Act, 1949. The document does not have an official "Guidelines 2022" numbering.

**Impact:** Annotators citing "Guidelines 2022" would appear less precise.

## Error 3: Incorrect IRDAI Regulation Name

**Location:** Section A5.3 – IRDAI Policyholder Protection Regulations

**Error Found:**
> "Policyholder Protection Regulations"

**Correction:**
The correct regulation is the **"Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India (Protection of Policyholders' Interests) Regulations, 2017"** – not "Policyholder Protection Regulations".

**Impact:** Annotators should reference the full correct name.

## Error 4: Incorrect Kappa Interpretation Threshold

**Location:** Section A3.3 – Inter-Annotator Agreement Statistics

**Error Found:**
> "Kappa >= 0.60 acceptable"

**Correction:**
According to **Landis & Koch (1977)** – the widely accepted standard:
- 0.00-0.20 = Slight Agreement
- 0.21-0.40 = Fair Agreement
- 0.41-0.60 = Moderate Agreement
- 0.61-0.80 = Substantial Agreement
- 0.81-1.00 = Almost Perfect Agreement

Therefore, Kappa >= 0.60 represents **"Moderate to Substantial Agreement"** – not simply "acceptable". The project requires **Kappa >= 0.80** (Almost Perfect) for production quality.

**Impact:** Annotators might have underestimated the required threshold.

## Error 5: Incorrect Product Classification

**Location:** Section A2.1 – Indian Financial Products Ecosystem

**Error Found:**
> "Digital Assets: Crypto (30% tax + 1% TDS)"

**Correction:**
The tax rate for cryptocurrencies is **30%** (Section 115BBH of Income Tax Act) and **1% TDS** (Section 194S). However, this is **NOT a "Digital Asset"** classification under SEBI/RBI regulations – crypto is not recognised as a financial asset in India.

The correct classification should be:
- **"Virtual Digital Assets"** (VDAs) under the Income Tax Act, 1961 (Section 2(47A))
- Not regulated by SEBI as a "financial product"
- Trading platforms are unregulated in India

**Impact:** Annotators classifying crypto as a financial product would be technically incorrect.

---

## Error 6: Incorrect NPS Withdrawal Rule

**Location:** Section A2.1 – Retirement Products

**Error Found:**
> "NPS Tier I: full withdrawal at 60"

**Correction:**
At age 60, NPS Tier I requires:
- **Minimum 40%** to be used for annuity purchase
- **Maximum 60%** can be withdrawn as lump sum
- There is NO "full withdrawal" option

**Impact:** Annotators who don't know this would miss compliance violations.
